Østfyns Museer asked us to create a powerful and learningful Virtual Reality experience of Nyborg Castle

Inside you find yourself within the Knights Hall of Nyborg Castle. Here you are able to interact with a miniature model of the caste comeplete with points of interest of which you can read and learn about.

You are also able to switch between the castle as it looked in the year 1200 and 1500 AD

"The quality of the product was far beyond expectations. Attention to detail and user experience was in focus"
- Nicolai Knudsen, Museum Curator, Østfyns Museer
