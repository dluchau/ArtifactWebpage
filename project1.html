Blacksmith

Our Viking Blacksmith VR Experience was created in collaboration with "Den Fynske Spilfabrik".

Made for a danish viking musem, the experience transports you back to the viking age, where you are able to forge your very own blade using a hammer and an anvil.

Heating up the metal and operating the bellows are part of what makes this experience realistic and informative.

The Viking Blacksmith has been a provision attraction in two different viking museums in the country. The game was originally developed for the HTC Vive, but has been ported to the Oculus Rift and further improved
